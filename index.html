01 · The situation

A renewal decision worth $1.3 million.

Hirschbach runs its contact center on 8x8 today at roughly $36,500 per month — about $1,314,000 over a 36-month term, billed annually and locked in.

The choice in front of the team is straightforward: renew with the incumbent, or move that same workload to Zoom Contact Center. Either Zoom path reduces the monthly run-rate. The question is how far you want to take the upgrade — and this page lays out both.

03 · The platform

What Zoom Contact Center brings to the table.

AI included, not sold separately

AI Companion — summaries, sentiment and agent assist — comes in every tier at no added per-seat cost.

Native video routing

Route and escalate to live video on Zoom's own media stack — a channel 8x8 simply doesn't offer.

One true omnichannel thread

Voice, video, chat, SMS, email and social on a single interaction history — pivot channels without losing context.

One platform, one admin

Phone, Meetings, Workspace and Contact Center under a single client and console — not a cobbling of acquisitions.

Low-code flow builder

Intelligent, skills-based routing and IVR your team can change in a drag-and-drop canvas — no professional-services ticket.

Transparent monthly billing

Payments due monthly — not a year locked up front. Predictable, line-item pricing with no reseller mark-up.
